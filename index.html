About Rotaract North

Who We Are Building Tomorrow's Leaders

The Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North is a vibrant youth-led community service organization based in Zamboanga City, Philippines. With over 75+ active members, our club is dedicated to service excellence, leadership development, and fostering positive change at the grassroots level.

Founded under the sponsorship of Rotary International, our club brings together young professionals, students, and community members who share a passion for making a difference in areas like education, health, environment, and youth empowerment.

Leadership Development

Empowering future leaders through hands-on experience and mentorship programs

Community Service

Making a meaningful and lasting impact in our local communities

Professional Development

Building professional networks and career advancement opportunities

International Service

Connecting globally while serving locally through international partnerships

Our Community Impact

Recent Projects Making a Difference Together

Discover how we're making a difference through meaningful community service projects and initiatives that create lasting positive change.

Health Initiative

End Polio Walk

June 30, 2025

The Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North participated in the End Polio Walk during District Conference 2025, raising awareness and uniting with fellow Rotaractors in the global fight to eradicate polio.

81 Participants
Community Unity

Free Iftar

March 23, 2025

The Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North partnered with Backride Pinas to host a Free Iftar event at Pasonanca's Butterfly Garden, celebrating Ramadan and bringing the community together through kindness and unity.

50+ People Served
Education Focus

Learn To Read

October 2024 - March 2025

The legacy continues as the Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North's flagship project "Learn to Read, Read to Learn", started its marvelous fifth (5th) year on October 5, 2024, and ended on March 9, 2025, at Salum Elementary School.

25+ Students
Health Initiative

Mi Sangre Tu Vida

April 15, 2024

The Philippine Red Cross hosted a bloodletting activity where Zamboangueños were invited to donate their blood for a cause. Held at Citimall, Tetuan, the Rotary Club of Zamboanga City North also provided free medical consultation with vitamins and medicines from Unilab.

75+ Blood Donors
Environmental

Roots of Resilience

January 11, 2025

Last January 11, 2025, the Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North and Rotary Club of Zamboanga City North joined hands planting 100 seedlings for ‘Roots of Resilience’—a mangrove planting project to protect our coastlines and nurture a greener future. Together, we’re building resilience for our community and our planet! 💚

100 Seedlings
Community Joy

Mi Pascua Y Tuyu Tambien

June 2024

For 20 YEARS, The Rotary Club of Zamboanga City North and Rotary Anns Club of Zamboanga City North has been a beacon of service with this project, and for 9 YEARS, we the Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North have proudly continued this legacy. This year, we brought joy to the children of Tolosa Elementary School through our annual project! 🎉
