About Our Club

The Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North is a vibrant community of young professionals and students dedicated to making a positive impact in our local and global communities. As part of the worldwide Rotaract network, we embody the motto "Unite for Good" through our commitment to service, leadership development, and fellowship.

Our club focuses on community service projects, professional development, and international understanding. We believe in the power of youth to create lasting change and work tirelessly to address the needs of our community while fostering personal growth and leadership skills among our members.
