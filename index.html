About Us

The Rotaract Club of Zamboanga City North is a vibrant youth-led community service organization based in Zamboanga City. With over 1,500 members and followers, the club is dedicated to service, leadership development, and fostering positive change at the grassroots level


Founded under the sponsorship of Rotary International, our club brings together young professionals, students, and community members who share a passion for making a difference in areas like education, health, environment, and youth empowerment. Through meaningful projects and partnerships, we strive to leave a lasting impact in our neighborhoods.
